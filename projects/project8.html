about.

This project investigates the potential of artificial intelligence in spatial design and architectural planning. By leveraging machine learning algorithms and neural networks, we developed a system that can analyze spatial patterns, predict user behavior, and generate optimized spatial configurations. The research involved training AI models on extensive datasets of successful architectural spaces, user movement patterns, and environmental factors. This allowed the system to understand and predict how different spatial configurations affect human behavior and experience.

Research.

The project demonstrates how AI can be used as a powerful tool in the architectural design process, offering new insights and possibilities for creating more responsive and human-centered spaces. The results show promising potential for AI-assisted design in creating more efficient, sustainable, and user-friendly environments.
