about.

This project explores the intersection of computational design and parametric modeling, pushing the boundaries of what's possible in architectural form generation. Through the use of advanced algorithms and computational methods, we developed a system that can generate complex, organic forms while maintaining structural integrity and aesthetic coherence. The process involved developing custom scripts in Grasshopper and Python to create a flexible modeling system that could respond to various input parameters. This allowed for rapid iteration and exploration of different design possibilities while maintaining control over the final outcome.

Process.

The process involved developing custom scripts in Grasshopper and Python to create a flexible modeling system that could respond to various input parameters. This allowed for rapid iteration and exploration of different design possibilities while maintaining control over the final outcome. The resulting models demonstrate the potential of computational design in creating complex, efficient, and beautiful architectural forms.
