TerracCurve Tower is an innovative architectural design that reimagines the traditional skyscraper through the lens of computational design and sustainable materials.

This project explores the intersection of parametric design, environmental responsiveness, and structural efficiency, creating a landmark that harmonizes with its urban context while pushing the boundaries of architectural expression.
about.

The TerracCurve Tower represents a new paradigm in high-rise architecture, where form follows both function and environmental performance. Through advanced computational design methods, the building's distinctive curved form emerges from a careful analysis of solar exposure, wind patterns, and structural optimization. The project demonstrates how parametric design can create buildings that are not only visually striking but also inherently sustainable and responsive to their environment.
